We run a lot of our day-to-day out of Telegram, and people keep asking how it's set up.
Here's the short version.

## What we actually use

- **A pinned checklist channel** for each move, so nothing falls through the cracks.
- **Reminder bots** for appointment dates and document deadlines.
- **A shared folder** of template messages — the emails and forms we send over and over.

## Why Telegram and not something heavier

It's where we already were. The lesson we keep relearning: the tool you'll actually open
beats the "proper" tool you have to remember to check.

We'll keep adding to this as the setup evolves.
