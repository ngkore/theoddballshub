Loans were the part we understood least and stressed about most. The good news: the
*shape* of the process is the same whether you're headed to Prague, France or anywhere
else. Here's the map.

## The rough sequence

1. **Work out the real number** — tuition plus living costs plus a buffer, not just the
   sticker price.
2. **Compare a few lenders** before committing. The first offer is rarely the best one.
3. **Get your paperwork ready** — admission proof, cost estimates, co-applicant docs.
4. **Apply, then push** — gentle follow-ups genuinely move things along.
5. **Read the repayment terms** properly. The moratorium period and interest treatment
   matter more than the headline rate.

## The thing nobody mentions

Interest often starts accruing before you've earned a cent. Knowing exactly when the
clock starts changes how much you actually borrow.

This one's general on purpose — the lender and currency differ by country, but the moves
are the same everywhere.
