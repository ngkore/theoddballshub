## Image & Media

- [imagecompressor.11zon.com](https://imagecompressor.11zon.com/en/image-compressor) — image compressor
- [iloveimg.com/crop-image](https://www.iloveimg.com/crop-image) — crop and resize images
- [pixelied.com — PNG to SVG](https://pixelied.com/convert/png-converter/png-to-svg) — good quality conversion
- [airbrush.com](https://airbrush.com) — AI image enhancer / text enhancer
- [pica-ai.com/photo-enhancer](https://www.pica-ai.com/photo-enhancer/) — photo upscaling and enhancement
- [image.pi7.org/passport-size-photo](https://image.pi7.org/passport-size-photo) — generate passport photos with a printable PDF layout
- [ezgif.com](https://ezgif.com/) — crop, convert, and edit GIFs
- [online-convert.com](https://www.online-convert.com/) — WebM to MP4 and other format conversions
- [redketchup.io/favicon-generator](https://redketchup.io/favicon-generator) — favicon generator
- **Inkscape** — free desktop app for vector images (SVG). Available at [inkscape.en.softonic.com](https://inkscape.en.softonic.com/)
- **Mopria Scan** (Android) — scan documents via a printer at high resolution: [play.google.com](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=org.mopria.scan.application)
- **kdenlive** — video editor for Ubuntu/Linux. Install from Ubuntu Software.
